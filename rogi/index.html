Ani

Roghaari Ani android application will provide a reliable platform to connect blood donors and patients who need blood as well as clinics and blood banks. People who require blood can use this app and locate blood Donors and make a specific request with just one click, it would alert authenticated donors of that blood group specified by the user within a 5km radius of the user’s location. Registered donors will get notification for the blood requests only if their blood group is compatible with the requested blood type.
